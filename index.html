FAQ

Questions answered

Is Profit First only for Shiprocket users? ▾
We currently integrate deeply with Shiprocket, which covers the majority of Indian D2C brands. Delhivery, Blue Dart, and Xpressbees integrations are coming in Q3 2026. Not on Shiprocket? You can still connect Shopify and Meta — shipping accuracy improves once your courier is added.
How is this different from Shopify or Meta dashboards? ▾
Shopify shows gross revenue. Meta shows ad spend. They never talk to each other. Profit First pulls all three — Shopify, Meta, and Shiprocket — and calculates your actual net profit after every cost, updated daily.
How long does setup take? ▾
Most brands are fully connected and seeing their real profit within 10 minutes. Shopify connects in 2 clicks. Meta and Shiprocket take about 3 minutes each.
My CA already sends me a P&L. Why do I need this? ▾
Your CA's P&L comes 3–4 weeks after the month ends. By then you've already run another month of ads at the wrong ROAS. Profit First updates daily — so you can fix problems while there's still time to act.
Will this change my ads automatically? ▾
Not today. Right now Profit First shows you exactly what to fix and why — with clear ROAS alerts, ad spend analysis, and product-level data. AI-powered ad execution is launching for Growth plan users in Q3 2026.
Is my store data safe? ▾
Yes. We use read-only access only. We never modify your store, orders, or ad accounts. Your data is never sold or shared.
Can I cancel anytime? ▾
Yes. Monthly plans, no lock-in. Cancel anytime from your dashboard. No questions asked.
